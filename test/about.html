Background

Rasmi was born in Kathmandu, Nepal as the youngest daughter in an upper middle-class family. She grew up in the city and moved to US at 19 to achieve her American dream. She always believed in helping others through social service by volunteering or working as medical assistant even with degree in computer science. During her initial years in the US, she had gone through work and school balance, relationship failures, financial hardship, misconception with their first and third pregnancy, weight gain and supporting her family. She loves exploring new places, do not hesitate to try new food, and experimenting with different cuisines in the kitchen. She quits her job to support her husband, his mother and their 2 children. She is deciding to make a comeback in the work culture by starting her own business.
